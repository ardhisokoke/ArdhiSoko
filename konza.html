About This Property

Kamakis Urban Residences brings contemporary apartment living to Kamakis, one of Nairobi's fastest-growing satellite corridors. Choose from 1, 2, or 3-bedroom units designed with modern finishes, functional layouts, and a suite of lifestyle amenities — all at an accessible off-plan price.

Located along the busy Kamakis–Eastern Bypass interchange, residents enjoy seamless access to Nairobi's CBD, Thika Superhighway, and the Eastern Bypass. The area is surrounded by established schools, shopping centres, hospitals, and a booming commercial scene that keeps rental demand consistently high.

Buy off-plan now and benefit from below-market entry pricing and flexible instalment plans. Units are expected to appreciate significantly upon completion, making this both an excellent home and a sound investment.
